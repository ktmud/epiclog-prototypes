Design Principles

Calm canvas, vivid spark. White surfaces let one colour at a time do the talking.
Each category, one hue. Coffee is tangerine, food is coral, countries are sapphire. The map of life, colour-coded.
Editorial typography. Italic serif for emotion. Mono for facts. Sans for speed.
Premium = restraint. Hairline borders, soft shadows, generous space. Nothing extra, nothing missing.
