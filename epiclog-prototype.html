Magenta carries the brand verb (log). Cobalt anchors trust and progress. Citrine is the celebration. Mint signals "done". Hard ink shadows give every surface a confident edge.

What's in this prototype

★Lists tab with Near Me row, swipe-to-pin/share/delete cards, progress rings.
★List Detail bottom sheet — drag the grabber to expand to 92%.
★POI panel — Notes & Place Details tabs with auto-save patterns.
★Backlog with AI grouping banner & per-source attribution.
★Explore with featured hero & community grid.
★Profile with shimmer journey bar, stat strip, achievements.
★Anonymous landing via the avatar in profile (toggle).

Try it

· Tap a list card → bottom sheet
· Drag the grabber to expand
· Tap a place row → POI panel
· Swipe a list card right → check-in burst
· Swipe a list card left → reveal pin / share / delete
· Tap + FAB → create flow
· Tap filter icon → sort & filter sheet
